## How to Get Started (3 Steps)

### Step 1: Know Your Login
You have a **first name** and **password** for this beta. They're formatted simply:

- **Email field:** Type just your first name
  - Example: `royce` (not royce@leap99.com)
  
- **Password:** `[yourname]-[suffix]`
  - Coaches: `royce-99`
  - Students: `roycestudent1-99` (NO SPACES)
  - Admins/HC: `louie-99`

The app auto-appends `@leap99.com` behind the scenes — you don't need to type it.

### Step 2: Log In
Go to **[http://localhost:3025/login](http://localhost:3025/login)**

- Paste your first name into the **Email** field
- Paste your password into the **Password** field
- Click **Sign In**

### Step 3: Explore Your View
- **If you're a Coach** (e.g., Royce): You'll see **L2** — your council and 6 students
- **If you're a Student** (e.g., Royce Student 1): You'll see **L3** — your goals and buddy pair
- **If you're Head Coach/Admin** (Louie or Kalod): You'll see **L1** — all 17 councils at a glance

## Quick FAQ

**Q: I forgot my password**
A: It's always `[yourfirstname]-99`. Ask your coach or contact Doc.

**Q: What does my "council" mean?**
A: Your group! Coaches have councils of 6 students + buddy pairs. Students belong to their coach's council.

**Q: Can I change my password?**
A: Yes — **Settings** (coming soon in Phase 2).

**Q: Is this LIVE data or a sandbox?**
A: This is **live test data**. Real coaches, real students. When you mark attendance, it's visible to your coach. When you message, they get it. So test thoughtfully! 😊

**Q: When do goals show up?**
A: Coaches will add goal statements in Week 1. Right now the page is ready but empty.
