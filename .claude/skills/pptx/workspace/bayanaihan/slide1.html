Executive Summary

BayanAIhan.net is a non-profit initiative to democratize AI in the Philippines through community collaboration. The immediate need is an AI-powered content generation system to promote the initiative on X and social media, with an urgent Launch Kit for the ISPSC Candon workshop on January 6-8, 2026.

Target Audiences

  • State University Students and Faculty
  • Volunteers and Contributors
  • General Public (Awareness)
  • Future: LGUs and MSMEs

Content Priorities

  • X Posts and Infographics (Phase 1)
  • Presentation Decks and One-pagers
  • Video Scripts (Future)
  • Automated Content Pipeline
